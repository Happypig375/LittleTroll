Play

Plays a wave (.wav) file.

Play:loop

Loops a wave (.wav) file.

Play:stop

Stops any audio emitted by the play function.

Play:systemsound

Plays a system sound. Avaliable sounds are: asterisk, beep, exclamation, hand and question.

Play:waittocomplete

Waits to complete playing a wave (.wav) file, instead of not waiting it to complete.
